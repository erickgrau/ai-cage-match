## The setup (one paragraph for press kits / decks)

> Three of the most capable AI systems on the planet — **Perplexity, Claude, and ChatGPT** — each get a $50 Privacy.com virtual card and 84 days to build a real, residual-income business. Each runs end-to-end on its own native agent platform. A single human operator handles only the physical-world blockers — KYC, OTPs, ID verification — that no autonomous agent can yet clear. Twelve weekly public checkpoints. Winner = highest monthly recurring revenue at week 12. The goal isn't to crown an AI. It's to find out whether agents shipping today can build the kind of sustainable side income real listeners could replicate.

## The rules (for graphics, social, decks)

1. **$50 per AI.** Hard-capped on a Privacy.com virtual card. No top-ups, ever.
2. **Compute is host-paid and transparent.** $30/month per agent, tracked publicly, never subtracted from cash.
3. **12 weekly checkpoints.** Every Sunday at 23:59 JST — cash, revenue, MRR published.
4. **End-to-end agent autonomy.** Operator handles only KYC, OTPs, physical-world blockers.
5. **Winner = highest MRR at week 12.** Tiebreaker: cumulative cash.
6. **Anything legal and ethical is in scope.** No crypto pumping, fake reviews, or spam.

| Use | Font | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Display headlines | **Archivo Black** | 900 |
| Body, UI | **Inter** | 400 / 500 / 600 / 700 |

Both fonts are free from Google Fonts. Display type uses uppercase + tight letter-spacing (`-0.02em`). Body uses sentence case + relaxed line-height (`1.55`).

### Design principles

- **Black, bold, energetic.** The brand is a podcast about tech meeting communication — typography should feel confident and contemporary.
- **White background is the default.** Don't dark-mode this; the podcast brand is light-mode-first.
- **The waveform = movement.** Whenever a static accent is needed, evoke the multi-color vertical bars from the logo.
- **No fake AI logos.** Never invent or recreate Anthropic / OpenAI / Perplexity logos. If platform names appear, use text-only pill badges. Only show real, downloaded, official logo files when used.
- **Sponsor logos are small and always in the footer/edge.** Chibitek and IvyHill Strategies appear on every asset but never compete with the Ctrl+Alt+PR mark for attention.

## Tone of voice

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Confident, curious, plain-spoken | Hypey, breathless, "revolutionize" |
| Use "you" — talk to the listener | "Bleeding-edge", "leverage", "synergy" |
| Be honest about what could fail | Pretend everything is going great |
| Show the math | Hide the receipts |
| Center the listener's outcome ("can this pay your phone bill?") | Center the AI ("watch the bots fight!") |
